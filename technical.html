COMPLIANCE & ENGINEERING

Engineered for Severe Climate Performance.

ASTM Testing Standards

All structural stone products undergo comprehensive ASTM laboratory evaluations. Our high-density Volcanic Recinto is tested under ASTM C97 for strict absorption and density metrics, ensuring minimum moisture retention to neutralize structural vulnerability during thermal shifts.

Freeze-Thaw Resilience

Canadian winters demand absolute material integrity. Our volcanic tuff selections are explicitly vetted for accelerated freeze-thaw cycles (ASTM C666/C67 alignment). This structural stability guarantees optimal utility from the damp coast of British Columbia to the sub-zero extremes of Alberta and Ontario.

Logistical & Import Compliance

We manage the friction of international supply chains. Every shipment complies fully with national Canadian standards, including mandatory NIMF 15 / ISPM 15 heat-treatment certifications for all timber crating to guarantee smooth, delay-free customs clearance directly to your project site.
